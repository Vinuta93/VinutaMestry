Birds - Graphic (Simplified Geometric Shape)

Concept

Birds have the fundamental right to live with dignity and fly in the sky without being kept in cages or subjected to cruelty. Even if we are not putting them in a steel cage, we have made cement cages for them by destroying their natural habitat. All the birds, designed for the personal projects, are illustrated in a square shape which resembles the concrete jungle that they are subjected to live in.
