Deloitte knowledge Center (DKC)

Brief Given -Wall branding for Deloitte knowledge centre spreads across two floors of a stand-alone building, which contains of 10 training rooms, four conference rooms, an auditorium, an amphitheater, and vibrant collaboration spaces with unique ideas and sculptures which sends out some meaningful message. Ideation - Tried to give a theme of Gurukul system as learning was imparted under a tree in Gurukul system. The tree stood as a symbol of wisdom pool. Pupils strived, attained, collaborated, guided to achieve wisdom and in a surrealistic sense, seemed to move towards the tree in harmonic motions. At the entrance reception, we have made 5 feet roots installation made by a rope which shows how DKC is the root for all our knowledge we gained from all the training at DKC.

Models with theme based abstract figures

The entire process for making these sculptures -

1. Person running/flying towards his goal to achieve his dreams.

2. Person clinging on the stand to show the dedication and patience to handle any situation and to stay firm. A person is reading a book and seeking knowledge.

3. Person standing on the ladder as a leader who climbed all the obstacles to reach that height.

Final output of all the sculptures created for DKC

Wall branding

Wall branding is designed with the theme of 5 senses with graphic elements keeping the touch of nature. To show 5 senses we have used the broken sculpture parts which depicts hand, mouth, ears etc.
